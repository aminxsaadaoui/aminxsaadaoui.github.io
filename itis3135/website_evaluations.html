Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple is a great example of a modern website that implements the principles of CRAP to work in its favor.

  • Contrast: Great representation of having lighter colors against darker backgrounds through photos.
  • Repetition: The website maintains consistent navigation, fonts, and design elements across all pages, which strengthens the user experience.
  • Alignment: An extremely symmetrical design that features positioned photos along a structured grid.
  • Proximity: Related elements, such as product descriptions and prices, are grouped together, overall effective design.

Lingscars

Lingscars is an extremely chaotic website that ignore the principles of CRAP, as well as the wesbite being overwhelming and an overall poor web design.

  • Contrast: Poor contrast coming from an blank white background with a hippie style margin along with bright green hyperlinks.
  • Repetition: Consistent repetition of unappealing gifs and elements throughout the site.
  • Alignment: The site is not aligned from having listings of vehicles in the center as well as in the margins.
  • Proximity: The links on this website are far too compact.
